About Our School

Founded in 1985, Xenosaga School has been a beacon of educational Xenosaga for over three decades. We are committed to providing a comprehensive education that not only focuses on academic achievement but also on character development and life skills.

Our experienced faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and innovative teaching methods create an environment where students thrive and reach their full potential. We believe in nurturing not just intelligent minds, but also compassionate hearts.

With a diverse student body and a strong community of parents and educators, we continue to evolve and adapt to meet the changing needs of 21st-century learners.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that empowers students to become innovative thinkers, responsible citizens, and lifelong learners who contribute positively to our global society.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing excellent education through innovative teaching methods, fostering critical thinking, promoting character development, and preparing students for future challenges in a supportive and inclusive environment.

Our Complete Profile

School History

Established in 1985 with just 50 students, Xenosaga School has grown to become one of the premier educational institutions in the region, now serving over 1,200 students from kindergarten through grade 12.

Academic Programs

We offer comprehensive programs including STEM education, liberal arts, languages, arts, and sports. Our curriculum is designed to meet international standards while respecting local values and culture.

Facilities

Our campus features modern classrooms, science laboratories, computer labs, library, gymnasium, swimming pool, art studios, music rooms, and outdoor sports facilities spanning 10 acres.

Achievements

Over 95% university acceptance rate, multiple national academic competition winners, championship sports teams, and recognition as "School of Xenosaga" for five consecutive years.

Faculty

Our team of 85 dedicated educators includes master's and PhD holders, with an average of 12 years teaching experience. Many are certified in specialized teaching methodologies and ongoing professional development.

Community

Strong parent-school partnership, active alumni network of over 5,000 graduates, regular community service projects, and partnerships with local businesses and international schools.
